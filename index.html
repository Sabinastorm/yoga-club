About us

Welcome to our yoga studio on Södermalm in the heart of Stockholm. We offer classes in Vinyasa Flow to everyone who is interested in yoga from begginers to advanced yogis. We believe that yoga is for everyone and a great form of exercise to stay fit and healthy for anyone who is intrested. Everyone is welcome to our yoga studio. No previous experience is needed for our beginners classes.
