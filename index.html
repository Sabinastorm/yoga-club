About us

Welcome to our yoga studio on Södermalm in the heart of Stockholm. We offer classes in Vinyasa Flow to everyone who is intrested in yoga from begginers to advanced yogis.

We believe in the healing power of yoga and that yoga is a perfect exercise form to stay fit and healthy for anyone who is intrested.

Everyone is welcome to our yoga studio. No previous experience is needed for our begginers classes.

Our classes

Vinyasa Flow for beginners. Suitable for everyone with little or no previous experience.

Vinyasa Flow for intermediate. Suitable for everyone with some previous experience.

Vinyasa Flow for advanced. Suitable for advanced yogis or anyone up for the challenge.
